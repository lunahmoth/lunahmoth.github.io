Professional Development Events


Latinos in Tech x Oracle Networking Event

The Latinos in Tech x Oracle Networking Event was a vibrant meeting of minds, where students and aspiring tech professionals gathered to explore the exciting opportunities within Oracle. Throughout the hour, attendees listened to insightful talks delivered by Oracle representatives, who shared their personal experiences and professional journeys in the tech industry. Interactive sessions highlighted Oracle's cutting-edge technologies, giving participants a hands-on feel for the tools that are driving today's tech innovations.

Participants had the unique opportunity to network with industry experts, gaining valuable career advice and making connections that could shape their future career paths. The event also provided a platform for students to learn about internship opportunities at Oracle, aligning their academic pursuits with practical, real-world applications. The event not only ignited interest in technology careers but also strengthened the bond within the tech community at UNC-Chapel Hill. It was a fruitful session, and we are excited to see how these new connections and insights propel our attendees into their future in technology.


Research Triange Park Latinx Career Fair

On January 26th, the Mountains Ballroom at NC State's Talley Student Union was buzzing with activity at the annual Latinx career fair. This event was a collaborative effort between UNC Latinos in Tech, SHPE chapters from NC State, Duke, UNC Charlotte, and UNC Wilmington Futuros En STEM. Attendees had the opportunity to engage with various companies, expanding their professional networks and discovering potential career opportunities.

Participants came dressed in their professional best, ready to make a strong impression on potential employers. The event provided a valuable platform for students and professionals to connect with industry leaders and explore diverse career paths. We were thrilled to see so many motivated individuals taking proactive steps toward their career goals. We look forward to the continued success of our attendees and can't wait to host more events that support career development and networking in the tech community.


Career Journey Panel

On April 8th, the Stone Center at UNC-Chapel Hill hosted a dynamic and enlightening Career Journey Panel featuring professionals from Accenture, in collaboration with Latinos in Tech at UNC-Chapel Hill, Mi Pueblo, and ALPFA Inc. Panelists delved into their personal and professional experiences, providing attendees with invaluable insights and strategies.

The speakers shared their journeys of overcoming imposter syndrome, emphasized the importance of navigating one's career with intention, and discussed the benefits of engaging with Employee Resource Groups (ERGs). They also recounted impactful experiences from their time at the University of North Carolina, which resonated with many current students. Attendees enjoyed not only the insightful discussions but also catered food and participated in an activity that featured a prize. We were thrilled to provide a platform where students and professionals could engage with industry leaders and gain perspectives that could significantly shape their career journeys.

Workshop Events


Minimal-Portfolio Website Workshop

On April 12th, participants gathered for the second part of our "Advance Your Portfolio" workshop series, aimed at enhancing online portfolios using the latest web development technologies. Attendees delved into advanced techniques utilizing React and TailwindCSS, learning how to give their project showcases a professional edge.

The workshop focused on practical, hands-on learning, and participants were advised to have Visual Studio Code installed on their devices to follow along. The session provided an excellent opportunity for those looking to impress recruiters and set their work apart in the competitive tech landscape.

Everyone left the workshop equipped with new skills and insights that are sure to transform their personal portfolio websites. We look forward to seeing how these enhanced portfolios will open doors for our attendees in their professional journeys.


Resume and Networking Workshop

Are you ready to take the first steps toward a successful career? That was the question we posed to Latinx students who joined us for our specially tailored resume workshop. This event, held in collaboration with Match IT Talent Solutions, provided expert advice and essential tools needed for crafting standout resumes.

Participants gained invaluable insights into highlighting their unique skills and experiences effectively on their resumes. The workshop was designed to ensure that each attendee could navigate the job market more confidently and make a memorable impression on potential employers. We're excited to see how our attendees will leverage their newly polished resumes to open doors to internships and job opportunities!
